DMAS (Dimethyl Acetyl Succinate)

About DMAS:

Dimethyl Acetyl Succinate (DMAS) is a specialty chemical with applications in pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, and other fine chemical industries. Its synthesis and availability at a commercial scale have been limited, making it a valuable compound for industries requiring high-purity intermediates.

Market Need:

  • Growing demand for DMAS in pharma and chemical industries.
  • Limited supply and high costs due to complex synthesis.
  • Opportunity for cost-effective, scalable production.

Our Contribution:

We designed and validated the chemical synthesis process for DMAS at a Pilot Plant Scale, ensuring feasibility for large-scale production.

Future Plans:

We seek investors and manufacturers interested in producing DMAS. Our offering includes:

  • A tested and validated synthesis process.
  • Proof of Concept (POC) for commercial production.
  • Support in integrating DMAS into existing chemical plants.
